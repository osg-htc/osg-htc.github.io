Upcoming Training Events

All User Training sessions are offered on Tuesdays from 2:30-4pm ET (11:30am - 1pm PT), on the third Tuesday of the month. The trainings are designed as stand alone subjects. You do not need to bring/have your dataset prepared before the training. The only prerequisites are some familiarities with using the command line inteface or shell. Having some familiarities with HTCondor job submissions are useful but not required.
